About Me

My name is Kyle Brittan and I am new to the web development field. Although inexperienced, I have a passion for learning and teaching and the field of web development and software programming has always fascinated me. As more and more technology becomes a greater part of our lives, it has become apparent that this field can lead to an almost unimaginable list of specialties in which a person with both logical and creative drives can express themselves.

I have approximately three years of undergraduate work that has been completed, primarily, at Glendale Community College and Arizona State University. Although I had once pursued and undergraduate degree in Political Science with an intention of law school, I believe that I will go a different direction, academically. Upon completion of this program with the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp, I will pursue a degree in Computer Science.

My employment history has been comprised primarily of supervision and management in transportation and logistics. I have also had the opportunity to specialize in Quality Assurance and Safety. This history has enabled me to acquire a variety of skill sets and experiences that will remain with me throughout my career as a professional web developer and software programmer.
